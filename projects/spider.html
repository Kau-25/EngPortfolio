12-Servo Spiderbot

Servo control · mechanical assembly

  • Built a 12-servo walking robot based on an open-source quadruped design, fabricating and 3D-printing parts in the makerspace with three degrees of freedom per leg.
  • Wired the power system: a battery pack with an on/off switch feeds two buck converters in parallel to distribute current across all 12 servos.
  • Converters and servos connect to an I/O expansion board paired with an Arduino Nano; an IR receiver on an analog pin handles remote commands.
  • Wrote firmware to decode IR signals and drive the servo gait sequences for walking and turning.
  • Mentored Zotbotics members through building their own spiderbots in workshops, then ran a competition with 8 robots competing in a race and sumo battle.
