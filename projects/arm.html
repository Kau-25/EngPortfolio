Mechanical build · assembly · makerspace fabrication

Overview

Built a full-scale 6-axis BCN3D Moveo robotic arm from the open-source design, fabricating and assembling every component in the makerspace.

What I built

  • Built a 6-axis BCN3D Moveo robotic arm from the open-source design, fabricating and 3D-printing parts in the makerspace.
